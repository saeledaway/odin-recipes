Egg Drop Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Chicken Stock
  • 1/2 cup Sesame Oil
  • 3/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/8 tsp sugar
  • 1/8 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp tumeric
  • 3 Tbsp cornstarch
  • 3 eggs
  • Scallions
  • Monosodium glutamate

Steps

  1. Bring stock to a simmer. Add sesame oil, salt, pepper, sugar, MSG and tumeric for taste and colour.
  2. Add a cornstarch slurry into the pot to change consistency to your liking.
  3. Beat the eggs and then while stirring add the eggs into the soup. How fast you stir determines the eggs shape.
  4. Serve!
