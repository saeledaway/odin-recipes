Shortbread Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 250g cold butter
  • 2 cups flour (or 1.5 cups All purpose flour, 1/2 cup rice flour)
  • 1/2 cup caster sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F/ 150 degrees C.
  2. Sift flour into a bowl, mix in caster sugar.
  3. Gradually work dry ingredients into the cold butter.
  4. Knead until dough has a very greasy texture.
  5. Press dough into 1 large square pan.
  6. Prick the top of the dough with a fork and then bake in the oven.
  7. Remove when golden brown (approximately 1 hour 15 minutes).
  8. If you wish, sprinkle the top with excess sugar while warm.
  9. Once cooled cut into pieces and share with your boss to enjoy.
